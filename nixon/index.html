About Us

Environmental Safety

As EMGIC We believe in transformative, participatory and inclusive global approaches in the realization of the SDGs. In this dream, we offer the technical and professional expertise and solutions needed in driving the agenda for environmental good.

  • Environmental advisory for development
  • Climate Change advisory
  • Climate Risk Analysis and Modelling
  • GIS solutions and Mapping
  • Participatory Planning, Monitoring and Evaluation
  • Midterm Review and End of Project Evaluation
  • Business Development and Proposal development
  • Start-up business modelling
  • Disaster Risk Management and Resilience Planning
  • Data Analysis
  • Green Growth, Climate Change Adaptation and mitigation for sustainable Rural Livelihoods
  • Environmental Impact Assessment/Audit, Strategic Environment Impact Assessment(SEIA) and resettlement plans
  • Biomass Energy Assessment and Surveys
  • Facilitating Training in green enterprises e.g. biogas, clean energy, agroforestry
  • Tree/Forest Husbandry Management
