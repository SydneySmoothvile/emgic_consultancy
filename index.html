Based in Nairobi, Kenya, EMGIC Consultancy Ltd provides consultancy in the areas of environmental monitoring, analysis, advisory and safeguards, Monitoring and Evaluation, Cooperative Development, Livelihoods, Climate Change, Bio-enterprises and Green Growth, and all GIS solutions in the cross cutting range in environmental management.


FEATURED PRODUCTS

We undertake the following range of consultancies and activities;

  • Environmental advisory for development
  • Climate Change advisory
  • Climate Risk Analysis and Modelling
  • GIS solutions and Mapping
  • Participatory Planning, Monitoring and Evaluation
  • Midterm Review and End of Project Evaluation
  • Business Development and Proposal development
  • Start-up business modelling
  • Disaster Risk Management and Resilience Planning
  • Data Analysis
  • Green Growth, Climate Change Adaptation and mitigation for sustainable Rural Livelihoods
  • Environmental Impact Assessment/Audit, Strategic Environment Impact Assessment(SEIA) and resettlement plans
  • Biomass Energy Assessment and Surveys
  • Facilitating Training in green enterprises e.g. biogas, clean energy, agroforestry
  • Tree/Forest Husbandry Management

THE FOUNDER


EMGIC supports sustainable economic development, meeting fundamental responsibilities in line with the Sustainable development goals. At EMGIC, we are alive to the emergent global environmental concerns of our time including climate change and risks, environmental degradation, pollution and biodiversity loss attributable to negative anthropogenic activities on earth.
